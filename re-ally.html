Work About Contact
Re-Ally

The Challenge

  • Create a platform catered to renters
  • Establish a renter persona
  • Design a dashboard for renters
  • Create a clickable prototype

Client

Re-Ally

Platform

Desktop

Software Used

Axure

Role

UX Designer / Design Lead

Research

We began our research by interviewing users who had found and rented an apartment from a website. We asked open ended questions to find out what their true goals were when shopping for an apartment. We also inquired about their relationship with their apartment manager. We wanted to know how they felt they were being treated. It was important to know how effective the communication was (from renter to apartment manager) throughout the entire process.

Persona
  • 14 User interviews
  • Followed two users through their apartment searches
The Solution
  • Create a way for renters to store all of their apartment searches in one location
  • Apply to multiple apartments at no extra fee
  • Feel connected with apartment manager and know that you can contact them at any time
  • Split your rent online with your roommate and know when your roommate has paid
Paper Prototyping

After researching and developing a persona, we felt confident moving forward with rough sketches. We quickly mocked up a few sketches that we felt catered to the needs of the renter. We then brought the sketches to our stakeholder and edited the design based on their business goals (all while keeping the persona's goals/needs in mind).

User Testing and Iterations

Upon agreeing on a design strategy with our stakeholder, we then put our design into a low fidelity prototype using Axure. Once we felt confident that enough information was included in our proto we then proceeded to test. We quickly discovered that users did not like to be overwhelmed with information on their dashboard, they wanted the top priority information only. We also discovered that users needed more visual cues to notice the amenities listed within the unit listings.

Final Iteration
Success Metrics
  • Track how many users click “Sign in with LinkedIn”
  • Track if users are taking advantage of applying to multiple units for free
  • Track how many users fill out their social security number
  • Track the traffic from third party sites, which sites are generating the most activity to Re-Ally’s
Next Steps

The next steps of this project would be to deploy on renters that are shopping for units and collect data on their searching habits.

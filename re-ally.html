    Research

    We began our research by interviewing anyone who had ever found and rented an apartment from a website. We asked open ended questions to find out what their true goals were when shopping for an apartment. We also inquired about their relationship with their apartment manager. We wanted to know how they felt they were being treated, how effective their communication was with their manager.

For nearly a decade, Veronica has spent her career focusing her creative energy in the fashion industry. Recent experiences with her family and friends suffering from potentially life threatening illnesses has pushed her to want to use her design skills for a greater good. She has witnessed first hand how a family member's work in the medical research field has benefited the community and pushed her to reach for her own goals.
